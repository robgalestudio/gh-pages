Beware the Bat Cave

March 23, 2021

With this piece I wanted to try getting myself more comfortable with Blender, so I challenged myself to start the process of designing the environment in 3D. I'm a little slow with it because I'm just getting familiar with the way it works, but I can see how it could help speed the process along once I know what I'm doing. The trick is to keep it simple, knowing that I'm going to be going over everything with more detail in Photoshop.

Once I had the basic block in of the environment and the lighting set up, I went into photoshop to add all the textures and the characters to the environment. As I often do, I found most of the textures here from a variety of photographers on Unsplash.
